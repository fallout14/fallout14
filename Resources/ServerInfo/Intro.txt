Welcome to Misfits: Nuclear Wasteland — an 18+ Heavy Roleplay server set in post-apocalyptic Utah.

You've emerged from your vault, crawled out of the ruins, or wandered in from the wastes. Wendover doesn't care who you were — only who you are now. Stay in character, fear death, and remember: the wasteland doesn't pull punches.

Press F1 to read the full server rules before you step outside. This isn't a TDM server — play a character, not a kill count. If someone's breaking rules, use Escape → Admin Help instead of taking it into your own hands.

Check the guidebook (F1 → Guidebook) for mechanics, factions, crafting, and survival tips. Our Discord is linked in the lobby — being in the Discord is required to play.
